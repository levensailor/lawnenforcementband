    LAWN ENFORCEMENT

    Est. 1995 — Suburbia, USA


    We are Lawn Enforcement. Born from the hum of Saturday morning mowers and the crackle of rabbit-ear antennas. Our sound is the dial-up handshake your modem never forgot.


    We play instruments found at garage sales and sing into microphones taped to broomsticks. Every song is recorded on a 4-track cassette recorder in a wood-paneled basement.


    MEMBERS:
